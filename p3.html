About Me

Hello! I'm T. M. Shamim, also known as Artman Sensei in the creative realm. I am a passionate developer and artist, blending hardcore logic with boundless creativity. My journey spans from engineering digital healthcare systems and building automated Robo Soccer bots to crafting intricate calligraphy and soothing landscape paintings.

I thrive on solving complex algorithmic challenges and exploring Machine Learning, while keeping a keen eye on clean UI/UX design. Whether I am writing code in C++, Python, and JavaScript, configuring servers on Linux, or holding a paintbrush, my goal is to create things that are both highly functional and aesthetically pleasing.
